## What this does

Shows all unpaid bills grouped by age — Current, 1-30 days overdue, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, and 90+ days. This gives you a clear picture of what you owe and helps you prioritise payments before they become seriously overdue.

## When to use it

Run this report when planning supplier payments, reviewing cash flow, or preparing month-end accounts. It helps you avoid late payment charges and maintain good relationships with suppliers.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Reports** from the main navigation
2. Select **Aged Payables**
3. Set the **As At** date — this is the date the ageing is calculated from (usually today)
4. Click **Run Report**
5. The report shows each supplier with their outstanding bills split across five age buckets:
   - **Current** — not yet due
   - **1-30 days** — up to one month overdue
   - **31-60 days** — one to two months overdue
   - **61-90 days** — two to three months overdue
   - **90+ days** — more than three months overdue
6. Click a supplier row to expand and see the individual bills
7. Use the totals at the bottom to see your overall liabilities by age
8. Click **Export CSV** to download the report for use in a spreadsheet or to share with your accountant
