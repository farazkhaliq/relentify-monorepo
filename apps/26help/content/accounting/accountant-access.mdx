## What this does

Sends an invitation to your accountant so they can log in and access your books. Accountants get full visibility of your transactions, reports, and settings without needing their own paid subscription. The flow works both ways — you can invite them, or they can request access.

## When to use it

Set this up when you first start using Relentify, or whenever you change accountants and need to transfer access.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Settings** and select **Accountant Access**
2. Click **Invite Accountant**
3. Enter your accountant's **Name** and **Email Address**
4. Choose the **Permissions** level (view only, or view and manage)
5. Click **Send Invite**
6. Your accountant will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation
7. Once accepted, they appear in the **Accountant Access** list with their status shown
8. To remove access, click the accountant's name and select **Revoke Access**
