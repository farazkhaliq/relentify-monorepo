## What this does

Calculates a health score out of 100 based on four checks: bank reconciliation status, missing receipts, overdue invoices, and VAT compliance. Each area is scored individually so you can see exactly where to focus.

## When to use it

Review your health score weekly to catch bookkeeping issues before they become problems, especially in the run-up to a VAT return or year end.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Reports** and select **Health Score**
2. View your overall score out of 100
3. Review the **Reconciliation** check to see if bank transactions are matched
4. Review the **Missing Receipts** check for expenses without attachments
5. Review the **Overdue Invoices** check for unpaid invoices past their due date
6. Review the **VAT Compliance** check for any filing gaps
7. Click any section to see the specific items that need attention
8. Address the flagged items and check back to see your score improve
