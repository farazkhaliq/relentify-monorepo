## What this does

Creates a mileage expense claim by recording the number of miles driven, the reimbursement rate per mile, and the start and end locations. The total amount is calculated automatically. If expense approval is enabled for your organisation, the claim goes through your normal approval workflow before it is paid.

## When to use it

Use this whenever you or a team member drives for business purposes and needs to claim mileage. Common examples include visiting a client site, attending a meeting, or making a delivery.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Expenses** in the Purchases menu
2. Click **New Mileage Claim**
3. Enter the number of **miles** driven
4. Check the **rate per mile** — this defaults to the HMRC advisory rate but can be adjusted if your company uses a different rate
5. Enter the **from** location (where you started the journey)
6. Enter the **to** location (where you ended the journey)
7. Add a description or note if needed (for example, the purpose of the trip)
8. Click **Submit**
9. The total amount is calculated automatically from miles multiplied by rate
10. If expense approval is enabled, your claim will appear as **Pending** until a manager approves it
11. Once approved, the expense is recorded in your accounts and ready for reimbursement
