## What this does

Shows every transaction posted to a specific account, in date order. You can filter by date range and transaction source to find exactly what you need, and export the results to CSV.

## When to use it

Use the general ledger when you need to trace a specific posting, investigate an account balance, or provide detailed records to your accountant.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Reports** and select **General Ledger**
2. Choose the **Account** you want to view from the dropdown
3. Set the **From** and **To** dates to narrow the period
4. Optionally filter by **Source** (e.g. invoices, bills, journals)
5. Click **Run Report**
6. Review the list of transactions with dates, references, debits, and credits
7. Click any transaction row to open the original document
8. To save a copy, click **Export CSV**
