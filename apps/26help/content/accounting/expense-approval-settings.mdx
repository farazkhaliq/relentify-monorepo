## What this does

The expense approval setting controls whether expenses submitted by your team need to be reviewed and approved before they count towards your accounts. When enabled, new expenses go into a pending state until an authorised user approves or rejects them.

## When to use it

Turn this on if you want oversight of staff spending before expenses hit your books. Leave it off if you are a sole trader or trust your team to log expenses without review.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Settings** from the left-hand menu.
2. Find the **Expenses** section.
3. Toggle **Require approval for expenses** on or off.
4. Click **Save**.
5. When approval is enabled, any new expense created by a team member will show as **Pending** until approved.
6. Approvers will see pending expenses on the **Expenses** page, where they can approve or reject each one.
7. If you turn approval off, all future expenses will be recorded immediately without a review step.
