## What this does

Locks a date range so that no new transactions, journals, or adjustments can be posted into that period. This protects months you have already reviewed and agreed with your accountant. An accountant override is available if a correction is genuinely needed.

## When to use it

Lock each month after you have completed your reconciliation and VAT return, or lock the full year once your annual accounts are filed.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Settings** and scroll to the **Period Lock** section
2. Click **Lock Period**
3. Set the **Lock Date** — all transactions on or before this date will be locked
4. Click **Confirm Lock**
5. Any attempt to post into the locked period will now be blocked with a 403 error
6. To allow a correction, your accountant can use the **Accountant Override** toggle
7. After the correction is made, the override should be turned off again
8. You can extend the lock date forward as you close each subsequent month
