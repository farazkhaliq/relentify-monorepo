# **App Name**: Relentify CRM

## Core Features:

- Core Contact Management: Manage contacts including landlords, tenants, and leads. Store essential information and link them to properties.
- Property & Tenancy Management: Add, view, and manage property details and associated tenancies, including start/end dates and deposit information.
- Communications Inbox: Centralized inbox to view and manage all communications related to contacts and properties, such as emails and messages.
- Basic Maintenance Tracking: Log and track maintenance requests for properties, assign basic statuses, and monitor their progress.
- Task & To-Do List: Create and manage simple tasks and to-do items related to CRM activities for improved organization.
- Audit Log & History: Automatically record all user actions and changes within the system to ensure full audit trails and accountability.
- AI-Assisted Notification Tool: Utilize AI as a tool to draft and suggest personalized rent reminders or maintenance update notifications based on system data.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated and professional blue (#226EC8) to convey trustworthiness and reliability for a business application.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#ECF2F7) to provide a clean and calm canvas that enhances readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet analogous turquoise (#13ADB5) to highlight key actions, alerts, and provide visual interest, contrasting well with the primary and background hues.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif. Its modern, objective, and neutral aesthetic provides excellent readability and clarity for extensive data and textual content within the CRM.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, vector-based icons that clearly represent their function. Prioritize intuitiveness and a modern, professional appearance.
- Implement a structured, organized layout with clear separation between modules and information sections. Focus on efficiency and data visibility for multi-tenant users, with responsive design for various screen sizes.
- Employ subtle and functional animations to provide visual feedback for user interactions, loading states, and state changes, ensuring a smooth and responsive user experience without distraction.
