Reapit has been a fixture in UK estate agency software for over two decades. It started as desktop software and has evolved into a cloud platform (Reapit Foundations) that powers some of the largest estate agency groups in the country. For enterprise agencies with hundreds of branches, Reapit's depth is difficult to match.

But for independent agents, small chains, and letting specialists, Reapit's complexity, long contracts, and enterprise pricing often feel like overkill. Relentify CRM was built specifically for this segment: agents who need powerful property management tools without the overhead of an enterprise platform.

## Pricing model

Reapit operates on a per-negotiator pricing model with annual contracts. Pricing is not publicly listed — you need to request a quote, and costs typically run from £100 to £200 per negotiator per month depending on the package and contract length.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Reapit |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Per-office flat rate | Per-negotiator |
| Starting price | £49/month | £100+/negotiator/month |
| Contract length | Monthly rolling | 12-36 months |
| User seats | Unlimited | Per-negotiator pricing |
| Setup fee | None | Varies (often £1,000+) |

For a five-person lettings agency, Reapit might cost £500 to £1,000 per month before add-ons. Relentify CRM covers the same office for £49 per month with unlimited users.

## Target market

Reapit targets larger estate agency groups — multi-branch operations with sales, lettings, and commercial divisions. Its feature set reflects this: complex workflows, extensive customisation, and deep integration capabilities that enterprise clients need.

Relentify CRM targets independent agents, small chains, and specialists. It provides the core property management features agents use daily without the configuration overhead that enterprise platforms require.

## Property management

Both platforms handle property listings, enquiries, and matching. Reapit offers deeper customisation of property data fields and workflows, which matters for large operations with specific internal processes.

Relentify CRM covers the essential property management workflow: property listings with full details, automated matching with registered applicants, viewing management, offer tracking, and pipeline visibility. The interface is designed for speed — agents can manage their day-to-day work without navigating through layers of menus.

## Contact management

Reapit's contact management is comprehensive, with detailed contact records, communication history, and relationship tracking across multiple offices and branches.

Relentify CRM offers contact management with full communication history, custom fields, segmentation, and the ability to track landlord and tenant relationships alongside applicant details. The key difference is simplicity — Relentify's contact management is immediately usable without training, while Reapit's requires onboarding sessions to navigate effectively.

Both platforms cover the lettings lifecycle. Reapit offers more granular customisation of workflows and compliance checklists, which matters for large operations. Relentify provides the same core functionality with less configuration required.

## Reporting and dashboards

Reapit offers extensive reporting with customisable dashboards, KPI tracking, and the ability to drill down into data across branches. For multi-branch operations, this reporting depth is valuable.

Relentify CRM provides dashboards with pipeline visibility, conversion metrics, revenue tracking, and activity reports. The reports cover what independent agents need to manage their business: which properties are moving, where leads are coming from, and how the team is performing.

## Integrations

Reapit has built an extensive integration ecosystem through its Reapit Foundations platform, connecting with portals (Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket), compliance providers, referencing services, and accounting software.

Relentify CRM integrates with major property portals and connects natively with Relentify's accounting platform, inventory management, and communication tools. The integration with Relentify's accounting suite is a differentiator for agents who want their property management and financial data in one ecosystem.

## Onboarding and training

Reapit's implementation process typically takes weeks and involves dedicated onboarding sessions, data migration support, and training. This is necessary given the platform's complexity but represents a significant time investment.

Relentify CRM is designed to be self-service. Most agents are up and running within a day, and the interface is intuitive enough that formal training is rarely needed. Support is available through live chat for any questions that arise.

## Mobile experience

Reapit offers a mobile application that covers core tasks like viewing management, contact lookup, and task management. The mobile experience is functional but reflects the complexity of the desktop platform.

Relentify CRM's responsive design works on any device. Agents can manage viewings, update properties, and communicate with contacts from their phone without needing a separate app.

## Contract flexibility

Reapit typically requires annual contracts, often with a 12 to 36 month commitment. This provides price stability but reduces flexibility if the platform is not the right fit.

Relentify CRM operates on monthly rolling contracts. You can start, scale, and leave without being locked into long-term commitments.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Reapit if** you are a multi-branch operation with more than 50 negotiators, you need extensive workflow customisation, you require deep integration with specific third-party services, or you have the budget and time for enterprise-level onboarding.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you are an independent agent or small chain, you want to be up and running quickly without weeks of training, you need powerful property management at a fraction of the cost, or you want the flexibility of a monthly contract.

## The bottom line

Reapit is a powerful platform built for enterprise estate agency. For its target market, it delivers significant depth and customisation. But for the majority of UK agents — the independents and small chains that make up the bulk of the market — Reapit is overbuilt and overpriced.

Relentify CRM delivers the features agents actually use daily at a price point that makes sense for smaller operations. Combined with the option to integrate accounting, inventory management, and communication tools through the Relentify platform, it offers a complete business solution that Reapit's per-negotiator pricing makes impractical for smaller teams.
