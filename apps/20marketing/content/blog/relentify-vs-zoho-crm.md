Zoho CRM is one of the most popular generic CRM platforms, known for its competitive pricing and broad feature set. It serves over 250,000 businesses globally and sits within Zoho's vast ecosystem of 50+ business applications. For many industries, Zoho CRM offers a solid balance of features and affordability.

But the property industry has specific requirements that generic CRMs do not address natively. Relentify CRM was built specifically for estate and letting agents, with every feature designed around property workflows rather than generic sales processes.

## The generic vs purpose-built divide

Zoho CRM's data model is built around leads, contacts, accounts, deals, and activities. These map well to businesses selling products or services through a sales pipeline.

For estate agents, the mapping breaks down:

| Property concept | Zoho CRM equivalent | Works? |
|---|---|---|
| Properties | Custom module required | Requires setup |
| Tenancies | Custom module required | Requires setup |
| Viewings | Activities (partial fit) | Approximate |
| Landlords | Contacts | Partial — no portfolio view |
| Tenants | Contacts | Partial — no tenancy link |
| Compliance | Custom module required | Requires setup |
| Portal feeds | Not available | Cannot replicate |
| Rent collection | Not available | Cannot replicate |

Zoho CRM can be customised through its Blueprint workflow builder and custom modules. But creating a property-specific CRM within Zoho requires significant setup time and ongoing maintenance. Relentify CRM provides all of this out of the box.

## Where Zoho CRM excels

Zoho CRM is a strong generic CRM with capabilities that serve its target market well:

**Sales automation:** Lead scoring, deal stages, pipeline management, and sales forecasting are well-implemented for traditional sales processes.

**Marketing integration:** Zoho CRM integrates with Zoho Campaigns, Zoho Social, and other Zoho marketing tools for lead generation and nurturing.

**Customisation:** Zoho CRM's Canvas design studio and Blueprint workflow builder allow significant customisation without coding. For businesses willing to invest the time, the platform can be shaped to fit many workflows.

**AI features:** Zia, Zoho's AI assistant, provides sales predictions, anomaly detection, and sentiment analysis.

**Ecosystem:** Integration with 50+ Zoho products means businesses can build a complete tech stack within the Zoho ecosystem.

These strengths are real — for businesses that sell through traditional sales cycles. Estate agents' workflows are different enough that these strengths do not translate directly.

## Automation

Zoho CRM's workflow automation is flexible and covers email triggers, field updates, task creation, and webhooks. The Blueprint feature allows visual workflow design.

Relentify CRM includes automation designed for property workflows: automated compliance reminders, tenancy renewal notifications, landlord communications, and arrears alerts. These automations work immediately without configuration because they are designed for the specific triggers that matter in property management.

## Contact management

Both platforms handle contact management well. Zoho CRM offers detailed contact records, communication history, and segmentation.

Relentify CRM provides contact management within the property context: contacts are categorised as landlords, tenants, applicants, contractors, or vendors, with relationship tracking that links contacts to properties, tenancies, and maintenance requests.

## Mobile experience

Zoho CRM's mobile app is well-designed and covers contacts, deals, tasks, and reporting. Relentify CRM's responsive design provides full property management functionality on mobile.

Both platforms deliver a good mobile experience. The difference is that Relentify CRM's mobile view is optimised for property-specific tasks: property lookups, viewing confirmations, compliance checks, and tenant communications.

## The Zoho ecosystem factor

If you are already using multiple Zoho products (Zoho Books, Zoho People, Zoho Projects), the integration between Zoho CRM and these tools has genuine value. Data flows between Zoho products more smoothly than between products from different vendors.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify Accounting, Relentify Inventories, and Relentify's communication tools. For property businesses, this purpose-built integration is more relevant than generic business tool integration.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Zoho CRM if** you are a property business that relies heavily on traditional sales processes (new-build sales, development marketing), you are already invested in the Zoho ecosystem, or you have the time and technical ability to customise Zoho CRM for property workflows.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you are an estate or letting agent who needs property-specific features immediately, you do not want to spend weeks customising a generic CRM, you need portal integration and compliance tracking, or you want flat-rate pricing for teams of four or more.

Zoho CRM is a solid, affordable generic CRM. For businesses that sell products or services through traditional sales channels, it offers excellent value. But for estate and letting agents, "generic" means spending time and effort building property-specific functionality that should come standard.

Relentify CRM delivers purpose-built property management at a flat rate that beats Zoho CRM's per-user pricing for most teams.
