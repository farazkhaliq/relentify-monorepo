Freshdesk, part of the Freshworks suite, is one of the most widely used helpdesk platforms. It offers a free tier that attracts small teams, and paid plans that scale up with additional features. It is a solid product that has earned its market position.

But Freshdesk has followed the same trajectory as many mature SaaS platforms: features that were once included get moved to higher tiers, and add-ons for channels like WhatsApp and phone increase the real cost beyond the headline price.

Relentify Connect is designed as a modern alternative — offering more at every price point, with transparent pricing and no channel-specific surcharges.

## Pricing side by side

| Plan | Relentify Connect | Freshdesk |
|------|------------------|-----------|
| Free tier | Yes (2 agents) | Yes (up to 2 agents) |
| Growth/Starter | £12/agent/month | $15/agent/month (~£12) |
| Professional | £25/agent/month | $49/agent/month (~£39) |
| Enterprise | £45/agent/month | $79/agent/month (~£62) |

At the Growth/Starter level, pricing appears similar. But the gap widens significantly at the Professional and Enterprise tiers, where Freshdesk charges nearly double for features that Relentify includes at lower price points.

## Automation and workflows

**Freshdesk** uses a system of dispatch rules, observer rules, and scenario automations. The approach is structured but can feel rigid, and the number of available automations varies by plan.

**Relentify Connect** offers a flexible rule-based automation engine. You define triggers, conditions, and actions — the system handles the rest. There is no limit on the number of active automations, even on the Starter plan.

Common automations include:
- Route tickets by channel, keyword, or customer segment
- Auto-assign based on agent skills or workload
- Escalate approaching SLA breaches
- Send follow-up surveys after resolution
- Tag and categorise incoming tickets
