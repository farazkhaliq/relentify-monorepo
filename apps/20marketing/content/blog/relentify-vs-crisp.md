Crisp is a French-born customer messaging platform that combines live chat, email, social messaging, and a knowledge base into a single tool. It has gained popularity with startups and small businesses for its clean design and competitive pricing — particularly its free and basic tiers.

Relentify Connect operates in the same space: a multi-channel helpdesk that brings all customer conversations into one inbox. Both platforms aim to simplify customer support for growing teams. Here is how they compare.

## Pricing

Crisp's pricing is straightforward, which is one of its strengths.

| Plan | Relentify Connect | Crisp |
|------|------------------|-------|
| Free tier | Yes (2 agents) | Yes (2 seats) |
| Starter/Pro | £12/agent/month | $25/seat/month (~£20) |
| Professional/Unlimited | £25/agent/month | $95/seat/month (~£75) |
| Enterprise | £45/agent/month | Custom |

Crisp's Basic (free) tier is genuinely useful — it includes live chat for two seats. But the jump to the Pro plan is steep at $25/seat, and the Unlimited plan at $95/seat is expensive for small teams.

Relentify Connect offers more features at every tier for less money, with the most significant gap at the Professional level: £25 versus $95 per seat.

Relentify's free tier is more comprehensive, including email ticketing, social channels, and a knowledge base alongside live chat. Crisp's free tier is chat-only with a 30-day history limit.

## Chat widget

Both platforms have well-designed chat widgets that embed on your website.

**Crisp's** chat widget is one of the best-looking on the market. It supports GIFs, file sharing, and typing indicators. The design is modern and friendly, and it is one of the main reasons people choose Crisp.

**Relentify Connect's** chat widget is similarly modern, with customisable colours, position, and behaviour. It includes pre-chat forms, AI-powered instant answers from your knowledge base, and proactive messaging triggers.

## Multi-channel inbox

Both platforms consolidate conversations from multiple channels into a single inbox. The experience is comparable — agents see all conversations regardless of source, with channel indicators showing where each message originated.

Crisp's inbox is clean and conversation-focused. Relentify Connect's inbox offers similar clarity while adding more structured ticket management features — priority levels, SLA timers, and assignment rules — that become important as support volume grows.

## Automation

**Crisp** offers chatbot scenarios — visual flow builders for creating automated conversation paths. These are useful for deflecting common queries and qualifying leads before they reach an agent. The bot builder is available on the Pro plan.

**Relentify Connect** provides both conversation bots and backend automation rules. The automation engine handles:
- Conversation routing based on channel, topic, or customer data
- SLA-driven escalation
- Automatic assignment and load balancing
- Follow-up and satisfaction survey triggers
- Tag-based workflow automation

For teams that need automation beyond the chat widget, Relentify's rule-based system is more comprehensive.

## CRM and contact management

Crisp includes a basic CRM with contact profiles, company data, and conversation history. It is not a full CRM, but it provides useful context for support agents.

Relentify Connect integrates with Relentify CRM for deeper customer data, while also maintaining its own customer profiles with conversation history and activity timelines. For teams that want CRM integration, Relentify's native connection provides richer data.

## Reporting

Crisp provides basic analytics on the Pro plan — conversation volume, response times, and satisfaction ratings. Advanced analytics and custom reports require the Unlimited plan.

Relentify Connect includes full reporting from the Starter plan:
- Conversation metrics (volume, response time, resolution time)
- Agent performance
- Channel breakdown
- SLA compliance
- CSAT trends
- Custom date ranges and filters

## Co-browsing and video

Crisp offers co-browsing (viewing a customer's screen in real-time) and video chat on the Pro and Unlimited plans. These are features that Relentify Connect does not currently offer.

For businesses that provide technical support where seeing the customer's screen would save time, co-browsing is valuable. For most general customer support use cases, it is a nice-to-have rather than essential.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Connect if:**
- You need SLA management, CSAT surveys, and custom reports at a lower price
- Multi-channel support including WhatsApp matters without paying $95/seat
- Automation beyond chatbots is important
- You want a knowledge base from the free tier
- AI features without premium pricing is a priority

**Choose Crisp if:**
- Video chat and co-browsing are essential to your support model
- You specifically love Crisp's chat widget design
- Chatbot scenarios (visual flow builder) are a primary need
- You are on the free tier and chat-only support is sufficient

## The bottom line

Crisp is an attractive platform with a beautiful chat widget and some unique features like co-browsing and video chat. For businesses that need these specific capabilities, it is worth considering.

But for comprehensive multi-channel helpdesk capabilities — SLA management, automation, reporting, AI, and knowledge base — Relentify Connect offers significantly more at every price point. The value gap is particularly stark at the Professional level, where Relentify costs a third of Crisp's Unlimited plan while including comparable (or superior) features.
