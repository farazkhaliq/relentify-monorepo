Alto by Vebra (now part of the MRI Software family after a series of acquisitions) has been a popular choice for UK letting agents for many years. It offers a solid set of features for property management, lettings, and compliance tracking. But the platform has changed hands multiple times, and many users feel that development has slowed while prices have increased.

Relentify CRM offers a fresh alternative designed from scratch for the modern lettings market, with more features at a lower price point.

## Pricing comparison

Alto's pricing is not publicly listed, which is common in the estate agency software market. Based on reported costs, Alto typically charges per user with annual contracts.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Alto by Vebra |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Flat monthly rate | Per-user |
| Starting price | £49/month | Estimated £50-80/user/month |
| Contract length | Monthly rolling | Annual |
| User seats | Unlimited | Per-user pricing |
| Setup fee | None | Varies |
| Portal integration | Included | Included |

For a letting agency with four staff members, Alto might cost £200 to £320 per month. Relentify CRM covers the same team for £49 per month with unlimited users.

The core functionality is comparable. Where the platforms differ is in the execution — the interface, the speed of common workflows, and the breadth of integrated features.

## User interface

Alto's interface has been through several iterations as the product has changed ownership. The current version is functional but shows its age in certain areas. Navigation can require multiple clicks for routine tasks, and some screens feel cluttered with options that are rarely used.

Relentify CRM was designed in 2026 with a focus on reducing the number of clicks required for the tasks agents perform most frequently. The interface is clean, consistent, and responsive across devices.

## Contact and landlord management

Alto provides contact management with communication history and the ability to segment contacts by type (landlord, tenant, applicant, buyer, seller).

Relentify CRM offers the same segmentation plus custom fields for tracking specific information relevant to your agency, automated follow-up reminders, and a communication log that tracks every interaction across email, phone, and messaging.

## Compliance tracking

Both platforms support compliance tracking for lettings: right-to-rent checks, gas safety certificates, electrical safety certificates, EPC ratings, and deposit protection.

Relentify CRM adds automated deadline reminders that alert the team before compliance documents expire, reducing the risk of missed renewals. The compliance dashboard provides a single view of all properties with upcoming or overdue compliance requirements.

## Portal integration

Both platforms integrate with major UK property portals: Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket. Properties can be listed and updated across portals from within the CRM.

The portal integration experience is comparable between the two platforms, though Relentify's portal feed management is simpler to configure.

## Reporting

Alto offers standard reporting on property stock, pipeline, revenue, and team activity. The reporting is functional but customisation options are limited.

Relentify CRM provides dashboards with real-time pipeline visibility, conversion metrics, revenue tracking by landlord, void period analysis, and team performance reports. Custom reports can be created without needing to export data.

|---|---|---|
| Pipeline overview | Yes | Yes |
| Revenue by landlord | Yes | Limited |
| Void period analysis | Yes | No |
| Team performance | Yes | Basic |
| Conversion metrics | Yes | Basic |
| Custom reports | Yes | Limited |
| Compliance dashboard | Yes | Basic |

## Integration ecosystem

Alto integrates with Goodlord for referencing, various maintenance platforms, and accounting software. The integration options are reasonable but have not expanded significantly in recent years.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify's accounting platform, inventory management, and communication tools. For agencies that want their property management, accounting, and operations on one platform, this native integration eliminates the need for third-party connectors.

## Mobile access

Alto offers a mobile application for iOS and Android that covers basic property and contact management tasks.

Relentify CRM's responsive web design works on any device, providing full functionality without requiring a separate app download. Agents can manage their entire workflow from their phone.

## Onboarding

Alto's setup process typically involves data migration support and training sessions, which can take days to weeks depending on the complexity of the agency's data.

Relentify CRM is designed for self-service setup. Most agencies import their data and start working within a day. Live chat support is available for any questions during the process.

## Contract terms

Alto typically requires annual contracts. Relentify CRM operates on monthly rolling contracts with no long-term commitment required.

## The acquisition concern

Alto has changed ownership multiple times — from Vebra to Dezrez to MRI Software. Each acquisition brings uncertainty about the product's direction, pricing, and support quality. Long-time Alto users have experienced periods where development slowed during ownership transitions.

Relentify is independently built and operated, with a clear product roadmap focused on the UK property market. There is no acquisition uncertainty affecting the platform's direction.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Alto if** you have been using it for years with established workflows, your team is trained on it and resistant to change, or you have specific integrations that only Alto supports.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you want a modern interface that is faster for daily tasks, you need unlimited users without per-seat charges, you want comprehensive reporting including void analysis and conversion metrics, or you prefer the flexibility of a monthly contract.

## The bottom line

Alto is a capable letting agent CRM that has served the UK market for many years. But its aging interface, per-user pricing, and the uncertainty that comes with multiple ownership changes make it increasingly difficult to recommend over modern alternatives.

Relentify CRM offers a fresher experience with comparable core features, better reporting, and a pricing model that does not penalise growing teams. For letting agents evaluating their options, it delivers more for less.
