Arthur Online is a property management platform that targets landlords and letting agents with a focus on operational management: maintenance, compliance, tenant communication, and financial tracking. It positions itself as the operational hub for property portfolios, handling the day-to-day management tasks that come after a property is let.

Relentify CRM takes a broader approach, combining property management with a full CRM for contact management, pipeline tracking, and business development — plus native integration with accounting and inventory tools.

## Pricing comparison

Arthur Online charges per unit (property) managed, with pricing starting from around £1 per unit per month. For small portfolios, this is attractive. For larger portfolios, the costs accumulate.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Arthur Online |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Flat monthly rate | Per-unit |
| Starting price | £49/month | ~£1/unit/month |
| Break-even point | 49 units | N/A |
| Contract | Monthly rolling | Monthly |
| User seats | Unlimited | Varies by plan |
| CRM features | Full CRM | Limited |
| Accounting integration | Native | Third-party |

For portfolios under 49 units, Arthur Online may be cheaper on a pure software cost basis. For portfolios above that threshold, Relentify CRM's flat rate becomes increasingly better value. A 200-unit portfolio on Arthur Online costs around £200 per month — Relentify CRM covers it for £49.

## Property management

Arthur Online's strength is operational property management. The platform handles maintenance requests, contractor management, compliance tracking, and tenant communications effectively.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Arthur Online |
|---|---|---|
| Maintenance requests | Built-in workflow | Strong — core feature |
| Contractor management | Basic | Comprehensive |
| Compliance tracking | Automated reminders | Automated reminders |
| Tenant portal | Yes | Yes |
| Landlord portal | Yes | Yes |
| Inventory management | Via Relentify Inventories | Third-party |
| Inspection scheduling | Yes | Yes |
| Key management | Yes | Yes |

Arthur Online's maintenance management is more detailed than most competitors, with a dedicated contractor network, job costing, and a workflow designed specifically for managing repair jobs. If maintenance management is your primary concern, Arthur Online's depth in this area is notable.

Relentify CRM covers maintenance management with request tracking, assignment, and resolution workflows. It is effective for standard maintenance needs but does not match Arthur Online's specialisation in this one area.

## CRM and business development

This is where the platforms diverge significantly. Arthur Online is a property management tool — it manages properties you have already let. It does not provide CRM features for finding new landlords, managing a sales pipeline, or tracking business development activities.

Relentify CRM is, as the name suggests, a CRM first. It covers the full lifecycle: attracting and converting new landlords, managing the lettings pipeline, handling tenancy management, and reporting on business performance.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Arthur Online |
|---|---|---|
| Lead management | Yes | No |
| Pipeline tracking | Yes | No |
| Landlord acquisition | Yes | No |
| Contact management | Full CRM | Basic |
| Email campaigns | Yes | Limited |
| Viewing management | Yes | No |
| Offer tracking | Yes | No |
| Business analytics | Yes | Operational only |

For agencies that need to grow their business alongside managing existing portfolios, Relentify CRM covers both sides. Arthur Online requires a separate CRM tool for business development.

## Financial management

Arthur Online includes financial tracking for rent collection, service charges, landlord statements, and basic accounting. It provides a good operational view of property finances.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify Accounting, providing not just operational financial tracking but full double-entry accounting, invoicing, expense management, and financial reporting. The integration means no manual reconciliation between property management and accounting systems.

## Reporting

Arthur Online's reporting covers operational metrics: maintenance response times, compliance status, rent collection rates, and portfolio overviews. These reports are useful for day-to-day management.

Relentify CRM provides operational reports plus business analytics: conversion metrics, revenue analysis, void period tracking, team performance, and custom reports. The reporting covers both the operational and commercial sides of a letting agency.

## Tenant and landlord portals

Both platforms offer portals where tenants can report maintenance issues and landlords can view statements and property status. Arthur Online's portals are well-designed and have been a selling point for the platform.

Relentify CRM provides similar portal functionality with tenant maintenance reporting and landlord dashboards showing financial performance and property status.

## Compliance management

Both platforms track compliance deadlines: gas safety certificates, electrical safety, EPC ratings, and right-to-rent checks. Both offer automated reminders before deadlines expire.

The compliance management is comparable between the two platforms. Both reduce the risk of missed renewals that can lead to legal issues.

## Block management

Arthur Online includes block management features for managing communal areas, service charges, and multi-unit properties. This is valuable for agents who manage blocks of flats or mixed-use buildings.

Relentify CRM's block management features are developing. For agencies with significant block management requirements, Arthur Online currently has an advantage in this specific area.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Arthur Online if** you are primarily a property manager (not a letting agent doing business development), you manage blocks of flats with complex service charge requirements, your portfolio is under 49 units and the per-unit pricing is cheaper, or you need deep maintenance management with a contractor network.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you are a letting agency that needs both CRM and property management, you want flat-rate pricing for portfolios above 49 units, you need native accounting integration, or you want a single platform for business development and operational management.

## The bottom line

Arthur Online is a strong property management tool that excels at operational tasks — particularly maintenance management and block management. For landlords and managing agents focused purely on operations, it delivers real value.

But most letting agencies need more than property management. They need to find landlords, manage pipelines, track team performance, and handle their accounting. Relentify CRM combines property management with these broader business needs on a single platform, at a flat rate that rewards portfolio growth.

The choice depends on whether you need a property management tool or a business platform. For most letting agencies, it is the latter.
