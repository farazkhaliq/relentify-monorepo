Jupix, now part of the MRI Software group, has been a popular estate and letting agent CRM in the UK for many years. It offers a comprehensive feature set covering sales, lettings, and property management. Like many established platforms in this market, it was built in an era of desktop-first software and has been progressively moved to the cloud.

Relentify CRM offers a ground-up cloud alternative that delivers comparable functionality with a modern interface and more accessible pricing.

## Pricing comparison

Jupix operates on per-user pricing with annual contracts. Exact pricing requires a sales call, but reported costs typically range from £60 to £100 per user per month.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Jupix |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Flat monthly rate | Per-user |
| Starting price | £49/month | Estimated £60-100/user/month |
| Contract | Monthly rolling | Annual |
| User seats | Unlimited | Per-user |
| Setup fee | None | Varies |
| Portal integration | Included | Included |
| Training required | Minimal | Extensive |

A five-person agency on Jupix might pay £300 to £500 per month. Relentify CRM covers the same team for £49 per month.

## Core property management

Both platforms provide the essential property management features that estate agents need daily.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Jupix |
|---|---|---|
| Property listings | Yes | Yes |
| Sales pipeline | Yes | Yes |
| Lettings management | Full lifecycle | Full lifecycle |
| Applicant matching | Automated | Automated |
| Viewing management | Yes | Yes |
| Offer tracking | Yes | Yes |
| Chain management | Basic | Yes |
| Compliance tracking | Yes | Yes |

Jupix offers deeper chain management for sales transactions, which matters for agencies doing significant volumes of sales work. Relentify CRM covers the core chain tracking needs but is particularly strong on the lettings side with tenancy lifecycle management, renewal tracking, and arrears monitoring.

## Lettings specific features

For letting agents, both platforms handle the full tenancy lifecycle. The differences are in execution rather than capability.

Relentify CRM provides automated compliance deadline tracking, rent collection monitoring with arrears alerts, landlord communication logs, and void period analysis. These features are designed for letting specialists who need to manage portfolios efficiently.

Jupix offers similar lettings functionality with the added depth of its property management tools, which extend into block management and commercial property. For agencies with mixed portfolios, this breadth can be valuable.

## Contact management

Jupix provides comprehensive contact management with detailed records, activity tracking, and the ability to manage relationships across property transactions.

Relentify CRM offers contact management with communication history, custom segmentation, automated follow-ups, and the ability to track every interaction across channels. The contact interface is designed for speed — finding and acting on contact information takes fewer clicks.

## Reporting and analytics

Jupix offers reporting dashboards covering pipeline, revenue, team performance, and property stock levels. The reports are comprehensive but can require configuration to show exactly what you need.

Relentify CRM provides real-time dashboards with pipeline visibility, conversion funnels, revenue analysis by property type or landlord, and team performance metrics. Reports are accessible without configuration, and custom reports can be created within the platform.

|---|---|---|

| Revenue analysis | Yes | Yes |
| Conversion metrics | Yes | Yes |
| Void period analysis | Yes | Limited |
| Landlord reporting | Yes | Yes |
| Team performance | Yes | Yes |
| Custom reports | Yes | Configurable |
| Market trend analysis | Yes | Limited |

## Portal integration

Both platforms integrate with major UK property portals — Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket. The portal feed management is comparable, with the ability to list, update, and remove properties across portals from within the CRM.

## Integration ecosystem

Jupix, through MRI Software, has a broad integration ecosystem connecting with referencing services, maintenance platforms, accounting software, and other property industry tools.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify's accounting, inventory management, and communication platforms. For agencies that want a unified business platform, this native integration eliminates the complexity and cost of third-party connectors.

## User interface and experience

Jupix's interface has been updated over the years but retains some design patterns from its earlier incarnations. New users typically need several days of training to navigate the platform efficiently, and the learning curve can be steep for less tech-savvy team members.

Relentify CRM was designed with an intuitive navigation structure that agents can learn in hours rather than days. The interface is consistent, responsive, and optimised for the tasks agents perform most frequently.

## Mobile access

Jupix offers mobile access through its web interface, which works on tablets and phones but was designed primarily for desktop use.

Relentify CRM's responsive design provides a seamless experience across all devices. The interface adapts to screen size, ensuring full functionality on mobile without compromising usability.

## Onboarding and support

Jupix typically requires a formal onboarding process with training sessions and data migration support. This can take weeks, during which the team is learning the new system alongside their daily work.

Relentify CRM's self-service approach means most agencies are operational within a day. Live chat support is available throughout the setup process and beyond.

## Contract flexibility

Jupix requires annual contracts, which provide price predictability but limit flexibility. If the platform is not the right fit, you are committed for the contract term.

Relentify CRM operates on monthly rolling contracts. Start when you are ready, and leave if it is not right — without penalty.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Jupix if** you are a mixed sales and lettings agency with significant chain management needs, you need extensive third-party integrations specific to the MRI ecosystem, or you are part of a larger group already standardised on MRI products.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you are a lettings-focused agency or independent agent, you want unlimited users without per-seat costs, you prefer a modern interface that requires minimal training, or you want the flexibility to integrate your CRM with accounting and inventory tools on one platform.

## The bottom line

Jupix is a comprehensive estate agency CRM with the depth that comes from years of development. For mixed sales and lettings agencies with complex needs, it delivers.

But for the majority of letting agents and independent agencies, Jupix is more platform than they need — and more expensive than it should be. Relentify CRM provides the features agents use daily at a fraction of the cost, with a modern interface that teams can adopt immediately. The pricing alone makes the comparison worth investigating.
