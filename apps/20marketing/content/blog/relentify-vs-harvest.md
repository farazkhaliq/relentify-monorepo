## Time tracking models

**Harvest** uses a timer and manual entry model. You start a timer against a project and task, or manually enter hours after the fact. This suits consultants and agency staff who work on multiple projects throughout the day and need to categorise every hour.

**Relentify Timesheets** uses a clock-in/clock-out model. Workers arrive at a site, clock in with GPS verification, work their shift, and clock out. The system records the precise start and end time. This suits hourly workers, field staff, and employees who work set shifts.

The timer model assumes workers are self-directed and honest about their time allocation. The clock-in model verifies attendance and provides objective records.

## Invoicing and billing

This is Harvest's unique strength. The platform generates professional invoices directly from tracked time and expenses. You can set hourly rates per team member, per project, or per task, and convert tracked time into client invoices with a few clicks.

Relentify Timesheets does not include invoicing. Its output is payroll-ready timesheet data — hours worked, overtime, breaks, and leave — exported for processing in your payroll system.

If your primary need is billing clients for project time, Harvest's integrated invoicing is valuable. If your primary need is paying employees accurately, Relentify's payroll-focused output is more appropriate.

## Budget and profitability

Harvest tracks project budgets and alerts you when a project approaches its budget limit. You can see in real-time whether a project is profitable, over budget, or at risk. This is valuable for agencies and consultancies managing multiple client engagements.

Relentify Timesheets tracks labour costs by worker, shift, and site. You can see how much a site costs to staff, identify overtime hotspots, and forecast labour expenses. This is valuable for operations managers controlling workforce costs.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Timesheets if:**
- You manage hourly workers who clock in and out of shifts
- GPS and photo verification matter for your business
