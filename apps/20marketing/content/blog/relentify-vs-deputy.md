Relentify Timesheets tackles the same problem space — managing when your team works and getting accurate time records — but with a different balance of features and pricing that suits small and medium businesses looking for more value.

Here is how the two compare.

## GPS and verification

Accurate time records depend on knowing that workers are where they say they are when they clock in. Both platforms address this, but differently.

**Deputy** uses GPS location to verify clock-in location. You can set up geofences around work sites, and the system flags when someone clocks in outside the expected area.

**Relentify Timesheets** uses GPS verification and also supports photo verification — a selfie taken at clock-in that confirms the worker's identity and presence. This dual verification is particularly valuable for businesses with concerns about buddy punching (one worker clocking in for another).

| Verification | Relentify Timesheets | Deputy |
|--------------|---------------------|--------|
| GPS location | Yes | Yes |
| Geofencing | Yes | Yes |
| Photo capture | Yes (clock-in/out) | No |
| Facial recognition | Planned | No |
| IP restriction | Yes | No |
| Kiosk mode | Yes | Yes |

## Break tracking and compliance

UK Working Time Regulations require specific break entitlements, and accurate tracking protects both employer and employee.

**Relentify Timesheets** is built with UK compliance in mind. Break rules are configurable based on shift length (e.g., 20-minute break after 6 hours), automatic break deductions can be applied, and the system flags when breaks are missed or shorter than required.

**Deputy** handles breaks competently but is designed for a global audience. UK-specific compliance features may require configuration rather than being available out of the box.

| Compliance | Relentify Timesheets | Deputy |
|------------|---------------------|--------|
| UK Working Time Regulations | Built-in | Configurable |
| Automatic break rules | Yes | Yes |
| Break violation alerts | Yes | Yes |
| Overtime rules | UK-specific defaults | Configurable |
| Holiday entitlement tracking | Yes | Yes |
| Right to work reminders | Yes | No |

## Timesheet approval

Both platforms support manager approval workflows.

**Deputy** offers a clean approval interface where managers review timesheets, make adjustments, and approve for payroll. The process is straightforward and well-designed.

**Relentify Timesheets** provides a similar approval workflow with additional features:
- Disputed timesheet handling (workers can flag disagreements)
- Bulk approval across multiple sites
- Approval audit trail
- Automatic escalation if approval is overdue
- Notes and adjustments with history tracking

## Mobile experience

Both platforms have mobile apps for iOS and Android. Workers use the app to clock in/out, view schedules, and request leave. Managers use it to approve timesheets, adjust schedules, and monitor attendance.

Deputy's mobile app is mature and well-reviewed. Relentify Timesheets' app is designed with a mobile-first philosophy, particularly for the clock-in experience — large buttons, GPS feedback, and photo capture in a workflow designed for one-handed use on a construction site or in a busy kitchen.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Timesheets if:**
- Photo verification for time records is important
- UK Working Time Regulations compliance out of the box matters
- You want lower per-worker costs
- You do not need advanced demand forecasting or AI scheduling
- You value the dual GPS + photo verification approach

**Choose Deputy if:**
- Advanced scheduling with AI auto-scheduling and demand forecasting is critical
- You need extensive payroll integrations (Xero, ADP, Gusto)
- You have complex, large-scale rostering requirements
- The team communication (news feed) feature is important to your workflow

## The bottom line

Deputy is a strong workforce management platform, particularly for businesses with complex scheduling needs. Its scheduling engine is best-in-class, and its integration ecosystem is mature.

Relentify Timesheets offers competitive features at a lower price, with UK-specific compliance built in and verification features (GPS + photo) that go beyond what Deputy provides. For UK businesses whose primary need is accurate time tracking with strong verification, Relentify Timesheets delivers more value per pound spent.
