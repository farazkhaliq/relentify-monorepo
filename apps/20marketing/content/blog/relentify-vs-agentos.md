AgentOS (formerly Letmc) is a cloud-based property management platform designed for UK letting agents. It covers the core lettings workflow: property listings, tenancy management, rent collection, and maintenance. For letting specialists, it provides a focused toolset without the sales-oriented features that dominate many estate agent CRMs.

Relentify CRM shares this lettings focus while offering more features, better pricing, and a more modern user experience.

## Pricing comparison

AgentOS operates on a per-property pricing model, where the cost scales with the number of managed properties. This seems reasonable until your portfolio grows and the monthly bill climbs accordingly.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | AgentOS |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Flat monthly rate | Per-property |
| Starting price | £49/month | Varies by portfolio size |
| Contract | Monthly rolling | Annual |
| User seats | Unlimited | Included |
| Setup fee | None | Varies |
| Portal feeds | Included | Included |
| Accounting integration | Native (Relentify Accounting) | Third-party |

For a letting agency managing 200 properties, AgentOS costs can escalate significantly. Relentify CRM's flat rate covers the same portfolio regardless of size.

## Lettings workflow

Both platforms are built around the lettings lifecycle, and both handle the core workflow competently.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | AgentOS |

| Tenancy management | Full lifecycle | Full lifecycle |
| Rent collection | Automated tracking | Automated tracking |

| Key management | Yes | Yes |
| Deposit management | Yes | Yes |
| Renewal management | Automated | Automated |
| Void period tracking | Yes | Limited |

The functionality is broadly comparable. Both platforms cover what letting agents need daily. The differences emerge in the details: interface quality, reporting depth, and the breadth of integrated tools.

## Property management depth

AgentOS provides good property management for standard residential lettings. It handles individual landlord portfolios, tracks property details, and manages the tenancy lifecycle from marketing through to checkout.

Relentify CRM matches this and adds void period analysis, landlord portfolio reporting, and the ability to segment properties by type, area, or performance metrics. For agencies managing diverse portfolios, this analytical depth helps identify which properties and landlords generate the most value.

## Maintenance management

Both platforms include maintenance request management. Tenants can report issues, agents can assign contractors, and the workflow tracks progress through to resolution.

AgentOS has a reasonable maintenance workflow that connects tenants, agents, and contractors. Relentify CRM provides a similar workflow with the addition of automated landlord notifications, cost tracking per property, and the ability to analyse maintenance spend across the portfolio.

## Financial management

AgentOS includes basic financial tracking for rent collection, fees, and landlord statements. However, it relies on third-party integrations for full accounting functionality.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify Accounting, meaning rent collection, fee tracking, landlord statements, and full accounting all live on one platform. This eliminates the data reconciliation issues that arise when property management and accounting are on separate systems.

## Reporting

AgentOS offers standard reporting on property stock, rent collection, arrears, and maintenance. The reports cover the operational basics but lack the analytical depth that growing agencies need.

| Report | Relentify CRM | AgentOS |
|---|---|---|
| Property stock | Yes | Yes |
| Rent collection | Yes | Yes |
| Arrears report | Yes | Yes |
| Maintenance spend | Yes | Basic |
| Void period analysis | Yes | Limited |
| Revenue by landlord | Yes | Limited |
| Conversion metrics | Yes | No |
| Team performance | Yes | Basic |
| Portfolio analytics | Yes | Limited |
| Custom reports | Yes | Limited |

Relentify CRM provides deeper analytics that help agency managers understand business performance: which properties have the longest void periods, which landlords generate the most revenue, and where the team's time is being spent.

## Contact management

AgentOS manages tenant, landlord, and contractor contacts within the lettings context. The contact management is functional but focused on the property management workflow.

Relentify CRM provides broader contact management with communication history across all channels, automated follow-up reminders, and the ability to track the full relationship lifecycle — from initial landlord enquiry through to ongoing portfolio management.

## Portal integration

Both platforms integrate with major UK property portals: Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket. The integration handles property listing distribution and updates.

## User interface

AgentOS's interface is functional and purpose-built for lettings workflows. However, it has not been significantly redesigned in recent years, and some areas feel dated compared to modern software.

Relentify CRM's interface was designed with current UI patterns and prioritises speed for common tasks. The navigation is intuitive, and the design is consistent across all sections of the platform.

## Mobile experience

AgentOS provides mobile access through its web interface, which works but was primarily designed for desktop use.

Relentify CRM's responsive design delivers a full-featured experience on mobile devices, allowing agents to manage properties, communicate with contacts, and track maintenance from their phone.

## Scalability

AgentOS's per-property pricing model means costs grow linearly with your portfolio. As you take on more managed properties, your software costs increase proportionally.

Relentify CRM's flat-rate pricing means your software cost remains the same whether you manage 50 properties or 500. This makes scaling more predictable and more profitable.

## Who should choose which

**Choose AgentOS if** you are deeply integrated with its specific workflows, you use its contractor network features extensively, or you are satisfied with the platform and the per-property pricing is manageable.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you want flat-rate pricing that does not scale with your portfolio, you need deeper reporting and analytics, you want native accounting integration, or you prefer a modern interface that is faster for daily operations.

## The bottom line

AgentOS is a focused letting agent platform that covers the operational basics well. But its per-property pricing model, limited reporting, and aging interface create an opening for modern alternatives.

Relentify CRM provides the same lettings workflow with better analytics, a modern interface, and flat-rate pricing that rewards growth rather than penalising it. For letting agents looking to modernise without compromising on functionality, it is worth a serious look.
