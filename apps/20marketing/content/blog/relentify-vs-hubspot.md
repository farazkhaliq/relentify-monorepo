HubSpot is one of the most well-known CRM platforms in the world, used by millions of businesses across every industry. Its free tier is generous, its marketing tools are powerful, and its ecosystem is enormous. For many businesses, HubSpot is a sensible choice.

But estate agents are not most businesses. The property industry has specific requirements — property listings, tenancy management, compliance tracking, portal integration, landlord relationships — that generic CRMs simply do not address. Using HubSpot for estate agency is like using a Swiss Army knife to build a house: it can do a bit of everything, but it excels at nothing specific.

Relentify CRM was built from the ground up for the property industry. Every feature is designed around how estate and letting agents actually work.

## The generic CRM problem

When an estate agent adopts HubSpot, they immediately face a configuration challenge. HubSpot's data model is built around contacts, companies, deals, and tickets. Mapping this to the property world requires custom properties, custom objects, and significant setup time.

**In HubSpot, you need to manually create:**
- Custom objects for properties
- Custom properties for tenancy details
- Custom deal stages for the lettings pipeline
- Custom reporting for property-specific metrics
- Third-party integrations for portal feeds

**In Relentify CRM, these exist out of the box:**
- Property listings with full details
- Tenancy management with lifecycle tracking
- Lettings pipeline with industry-standard stages
- Portal integration with Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket
- Compliance tracking with automated reminders

The time spent configuring HubSpot to work for property is time that could be spent using a platform that already works for property.

## Where HubSpot genuinely excels

HubSpot's strengths are in marketing automation and sales engagement for businesses that sell products or services through digital channels.

**HubSpot is excellent at:**
- Email marketing campaigns and automation
- Content management and blogging
- Lead scoring and nurturing
- Sales email sequences
- Meeting scheduling
- Website analytics

For businesses that generate leads through content marketing and nurture them through email sequences, HubSpot's marketing tools are best-in-class. But estate agents generate leads through property portals, walk-ins, and referrals — not through blog-driven inbound marketing funnels.

## The customisation trap

Some agents attempt to customise HubSpot to work for property. This involves:

1. Creating custom objects for properties (requires Operations Hub, starting at £711/month)
2. Building custom deal pipelines for sales and lettings
3. Setting up custom properties for tenancy details, compliance dates, and property attributes
4. Building custom reports and dashboards
5. Finding third-party integrations for portal feeds (often unreliable)
6. Ongoing maintenance as HubSpot updates break custom configurations

This customisation project can take weeks to months and requires either HubSpot consultancy or an in-house developer. The ongoing maintenance cost is often underestimated — every HubSpot update can break custom configurations.

## Reporting

HubSpot's reporting is powerful for generic business metrics: deal velocity, conversion rates, email open rates, and revenue attribution. But it cannot report on property-specific metrics without extensive custom setup.

Relentify CRM reports on what agents actually need: pipeline by property type, void period analysis, compliance status, landlord portfolio performance, conversion from viewing to offer, and team performance on property-specific tasks.

## Who should choose which

**Choose HubSpot if** you are running a property business that relies heavily on digital marketing (content, SEO, email campaigns) and the CRM aspects are secondary to the marketing automation, or you have the budget and technical resources to customise HubSpot for property workflows.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you need a CRM that works for property out of the box, you want portal integration and tenancy management without custom development, you need compliance tracking and property-specific reporting, or your budget does not stretch to HubSpot's professional-tier pricing.
