Zendesk's pricing has increased steadily over the years, with many features that were once standard now locked behind premium tiers. For small and medium businesses, the cost can be difficult to justify — particularly when newer platforms offer the same capabilities (and more) at a fraction of the price.

Relentify Connect is built to be that alternative: a modern, multi-channel helpdesk that includes more features at every tier and charges less for them.

## Automation

Automation is where helpdesks save time and ensure consistency. Both platforms offer automation, but accessibility differs.

**Zendesk** separates automation into triggers, automations, and macros. Triggers fire on ticket events, automations run on time-based conditions, and macros are manual shortcuts. The system is powerful but has a learning curve, and the number of available automations is limited on lower tiers.

**Relentify Connect** provides a unified automation engine. You create rules with conditions and actions — if a ticket meets these criteria, do these things. There is no artificial limit on the number of automations, even on the Starter plan.

Common automations that work out of the box include:
- Auto-assign tickets based on channel, topic, or keywords
- Escalate tickets approaching SLA breach
- Send CSAT surveys after ticket resolution
- Tag and categorise tickets automatically
- Notify managers of high-priority tickets

## AI and automation

Both platforms are investing in AI features, though the approaches differ.

**Zendesk** offers AI-powered features through its Advanced AI add-on, which costs extra on top of your plan. Features include intelligent triage, suggested replies, and content cues for knowledge base improvements.

**Relentify Connect** includes AI features as part of the platform — suggested replies, automatic ticket categorisation, and knowledge base article suggestions are available without an additional AI surcharge.
