Zoho Desk is the helpdesk arm of the Zoho ecosystem — a sprawling suite of 50+ business applications covering everything from CRM to accounting to project management. Zoho's strategy is clear: offer everything a business needs, keep prices competitive, and make switching costs high by keeping you within the ecosystem.

For businesses already using Zoho CRM, Zoho Books, or other Zoho products, Zoho Desk is the natural helpdesk choice. But if you are evaluating helpdesk software on its own merits — features, usability, and value — how does it compare to Relentify Connect?

## Pricing

Zoho Desk is competitively priced, which is one of its main selling points.

| Plan | Relentify Connect | Zoho Desk |
|------|------------------|-----------|
| Free tier | Yes (2 agents) | Yes (3 agents) |
| Starter/Standard | £12/agent/month | $14/agent/month (~£11) |
| Professional | £25/agent/month | $23/agent/month (~£18) |
| Enterprise | £45/agent/month | $40/agent/month (~£31) |

At headline prices, Zoho Desk is slightly cheaper on some tiers. But pricing alone does not tell the full story — what matters is what you get for that price and how the product actually performs in daily use.

## The ecosystem question

Zoho's biggest advantage is its ecosystem. If you use Zoho CRM, the integration with Zoho Desk is seamless. Customer data flows between products, and you get a unified view of the customer across sales and support.

If you are not in the Zoho ecosystem, this advantage disappears. And being in the Zoho ecosystem has its own trade-offs: the interfaces across Zoho products can feel inconsistent, with varying levels of design quality and usability.

Relentify Connect integrates with Relentify's other products — CRM, accounting, inventory — but also provides API access and webhook support for connecting to non-Relentify tools. You are not locked into an ecosystem.

## User experience

This is where the comparison gets subjective but important.

**Zoho Desk** is functional but can feel cluttered. The interface carries the weight of many features and configuration options, which makes it powerful but sometimes overwhelming. New agents typically need more onboarding time to become productive.

**Relentify Connect** is designed with a cleaner, more modern interface. The focus is on making common tasks fast — opening a conversation, viewing customer context, replying, and moving on. Power features are accessible but not in the way of everyday workflow.

For teams that value agent productivity and low training time, the user experience difference matters.

## Automation

**Zoho Desk** offers workflow rules, macros, and time-based rules. The system is capable but has limits on the Standard plan — only 5 workflow rules per module, which can be restrictive for teams with complex routing needs.

**Relentify Connect** does not limit the number of automation rules on any plan. You can create as many automations as your workflow requires:
- Route conversations by channel, topic, or customer segment
- Auto-assign based on agent availability or expertise
- Escalate on SLA breach
- Send automatic follow-ups and surveys
- Tag, categorise, and prioritise automatically

## AI features

Zoho offers Zia, its AI assistant, which provides features like sentiment analysis, response suggestions, and anomaly detection. However, Zia is only available on the Enterprise plan ($40/agent/month).

Relentify Connect includes AI features on all paid plans:
- Suggested replies from knowledge base content
- Automatic ticket categorisation
- Conversation summaries
- Priority suggestions based on content analysis

For teams that want AI to enhance their support workflow, Relentify provides access without requiring the most expensive plan.

## Migration

Relentify Connect supports importing data from Zoho Desk, including tickets, contacts, and knowledge base articles. For teams considering a switch, the migration preserves history and customer context.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Connect if:**
- You want live chat, social channels, and AI without upgrading to premium tiers
- User experience and agent productivity are priorities
- You want unlimited automations without per-module caps
- You are not already invested in the Zoho ecosystem
- You want full reporting on every paid plan

**Choose Zoho Desk if:**
- You are already using Zoho CRM, Zoho Books, or other Zoho products
- The ecosystem integration matters more than standalone features
- Community forums are important to your support model
- You need the absolute lowest per-agent cost and can work within feature limits

## The bottom line

Zoho Desk is a capable helpdesk at a competitive price — on paper. But when you add up the cost of the features most support teams actually need — live chat, social channels, custom reports, AI — the Professional plan is where most teams end up, and the price advantage narrows considerably.

Relentify Connect includes more features at every tier, offers a cleaner user experience, and does not lock essential capabilities behind premium plans. For teams evaluating helpdesk software on its own merits rather than ecosystem loyalty, Relentify Connect is the stronger choice.
