LiveChat is one of the most established live chat platforms on the market. Founded in 2002, it has built a reputation for reliability, a polished interface, and strong features. It is used by over 37,000 businesses and has earned its position as a premium chat tool.

But premium comes at a premium price. LiveChat's Starter plan begins at $20/agent/month, and the features most businesses want — like chat routing, reports, and integrations — require the Team plan at $41/agent/month or higher. For a team of five agents, that is over $200/month for live chat alone.

Relentify Chat offers the same quality of features — including some that LiveChat charges extra for — completely free. Here is the full comparison.

## Integrations

LiveChat has an extensive marketplace with 200+ integrations. This is a genuine strength — if you need LiveChat to connect to a specific CRM, helpdesk, or e-commerce platform, there is likely an integration available.

Relentify Chat integrates natively with Relentify's ecosystem and provides API and webhook support for custom integrations. The integration library is smaller but growing, and the API enables connection to most platforms.

For businesses that depend on specific LiveChat integrations, this could be a deciding factor. For everyone else, the available integrations and API access are sufficient.

## E-commerce features

LiveChat has strong e-commerce integrations — particularly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce. You can see what visitors have in their cart, recommend products, and track sales attributed to chat.

Relentify Chat does not focus as heavily on e-commerce-specific features. For online retailers where chat-driven sales are a primary goal, LiveChat's e-commerce depth may be more relevant.
