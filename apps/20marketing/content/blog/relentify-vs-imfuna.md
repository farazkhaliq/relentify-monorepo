Imfuna has carved out a niche in the property inspection software market, particularly in South Africa and the UK. Their app allows property professionals to create detailed inspection reports using a smartphone or tablet, with a focus on photo documentation and standardised condition assessments.

Relentify Inventories tackles the same challenge — helping letting agents, landlords, and inventory clerks produce professional property reports — but with a more modern platform, more generous pricing, and features designed around the realities of managing a property portfolio.

Here is how the two compare across the areas that matter most.

## Platform and accessibility

| Aspect | Relentify Inventories | Imfuna |
|--------|----------------------|--------|
| Mobile app | iOS and Android | iOS and Android |
| Web dashboard | Full-featured | Yes |
| Offline support | Yes | Yes |
| Multi-device sync | Automatic | Yes |
| Minimum OS version | Modern (iOS 15+, Android 10+) | Varies |

Both platforms work on smartphones and tablets, which is essential for conducting inspections in the field. Both support offline operation — you can complete an entire inspection without a network connection and sync later.

Relentify's web dashboard provides a more comprehensive management experience, with property portfolios, tenancy timelines, and team management all accessible from a browser. Imfuna's web interface focuses primarily on report management and delivery.

## Report types and workflow

Property professionals need different types of reports at different stages of a tenancy. Here is what each platform supports:

| Report type | Relentify Inventories | Imfuna |
|-------------|----------------------|--------|
| Check-in inventory | Yes | Yes |
| Check-out inventory | Yes | Yes |
| Mid-tenancy inspection | Yes | Yes |
| Interim inspection | Yes | Yes |
| Schedule of condition | Yes | Yes |
| Building survey | No | Yes |
| Comparative report | Automatic | Manual |

Imfuna casts a wider net with building surveys, which appeals to commercial property professionals. Relentify focuses specifically on residential lettings, which means the features are tailored and streamlined for that use case rather than being a compromise across multiple property sectors.

The most significant workflow difference is in comparative reports. When you create a check-out report in Relentify, the check-in data is loaded automatically. The software generates a comparison highlighting changes in condition, missing items, and new damage — all with side-by-side photos. This saves considerable time and reduces the risk of missing a detail that could affect a deposit claim.

Imfuna supports check-out reports that reference the original inventory, but the comparison process is more manual.

## Photo documentation

Both platforms treat photos as essential evidence. The differences are in how they handle metadata and volume.

| Photo feature | Relentify Inventories | Imfuna |
|---------------|----------------------|--------|
| In-app camera | Yes | Yes |
| Timestamped | Yes (automatic) | Yes |
| GPS-tagged | Yes (automatic) | Yes |
| Photo annotations | Yes (draw, text, arrows) | Basic |
| Photos per item | Unlimited | Varies |
| Photo quality | High resolution preserved | Compressed |
| Bulk capture mode | Yes | Limited |

Relentify preserves photo quality at higher resolution, which matters when zooming into details during a dispute — is that a scratch or a shadow? With heavy compression, fine details can be lost.

The annotation tools in Relentify are also more developed. You can draw circles around damage, add text labels, and use arrows to point out specific areas. This makes reports clearer for landlords, tenants, and adjudicators who may not have visited the property themselves.

## Condition assessments

How condition is recorded and communicated can make or break a deposit dispute case.

**Imfuna** uses a condition rating system that allows clerks to grade items on a scale, typically with accompanying descriptions. The system is functional and widely understood.

**Relentify** uses a standardised numerical scale with clear definitions for each grade. Every report uses the same scale, which means there is no ambiguity when comparing a check-in graded by one team member with a check-out graded by another. The consistency strengthens your position in any adjudication process.

Imfuna's pricing is less transparent — you typically need to contact them for a quote, which can make it harder to compare costs upfront. Relentify publishes pricing openly, with unlimited reports on all paid plans and no per-report surcharges.

For a busy inventory clerk conducting 20 or more inspections per month, the difference between unlimited and per-report pricing is substantial. With Relentify, your costs are predictable regardless of how busy you are.

## Team management

If you run an inventory business or manage a letting agency with multiple inspectors, team features matter.

| Team feature | Relentify Inventories | Imfuna |
|--------------|----------------------|--------|
| Multiple inspectors | Yes | Yes |
| Role-based access | Yes | Limited |
| Work allocation | Built-in | Basic |
| Quality review | Yes (before publishing) | Yes |

Relentify provides a manager dashboard where you can see which inspections are in progress, which are complete, and review reports before they are sent to clients. This quality control step is valuable for agencies that want to maintain consistent standards across their team.

## Integration and data management

| Data feature | Relentify Inventories | Imfuna |
|--------------|----------------------|--------|
| Cloud storage | Yes (all plans) | Yes |
| Property history | Full timeline | Basic history |
| Search and filter | Advanced | Basic |
| Data export | PDF, CSV | PDF |
| API access | Available | Limited |
| Bulk operations | Yes | Limited |

Relentify stores every inspection as part of a property timeline, making it easy to see the full history across multiple tenancies. This is particularly useful for agents managing properties over years — you can quickly pull up any previous report without digging through emails or filing systems.

## Report presentation

The final report is what landlords, tenants, and adjudicators see. Presentation matters.

**Relentify** generates clean, professionally formatted PDFs with your agency branding, high-resolution photos, clear condition ratings, and a logical room-by-room structure. Reports are generated instantly on the device — no waiting for server-side processing.

**Imfuna** also produces professional reports, though the formatting is more standardised with less customisation available for branding and layout. Report generation may require syncing to the cloud before the final PDF is available.

## Regional considerations

Imfuna has a strong presence in South Africa and is expanding in the UK market. Their software reflects practices common across multiple regions.

Relentify is built primarily for the UK lettings market, which means features like TDS/DPS compliance considerations, fair wear and tear guidance, and UK-standard condition scales are baked into the product rather than being adaptations of a multi-market tool.

For UK-based agents and clerks, this focus means fewer irrelevant features and more alignment with how the UK deposit protection schemes actually work.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify if:**
- You want transparent, predictable pricing with unlimited reports
- You work primarily in UK residential lettings
- Automatic comparison reports would save you time
- You want instant on-site report generation
- Photo quality and annotation tools are important
- You need team management features without per-user charges

**Choose Imfuna if:**
- You work across multiple property sectors including commercial
- You need building survey capability alongside inventory
- You are already embedded in the Imfuna ecosystem
- You operate primarily outside the UK

## The bottom line

Both Relentify and Imfuna are capable inspection platforms. Imfuna has the advantage of a longer track record and broader property sector coverage. Relentify has the advantage of modern design, more generous pricing, automatic comparative reports, and features specifically tailored for UK residential lettings.

For most letting agents and inventory clerks working in the UK market, Relentify offers more of what you need at a lower price — with the added benefit of a platform that is being actively developed with your specific workflow in mind.
