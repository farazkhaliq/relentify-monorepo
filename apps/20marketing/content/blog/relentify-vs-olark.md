Olark has been in the live chat market since 2009. It positions itself as simple, human-focused live chat — a tool for real conversations between real people. No chatbots, no complex automation, just straightforward chat with a clean interface and reliable performance.

That simplicity is appealing. But in 2027, most businesses want more than basic live chat. They want AI-powered responses for when agents are offline, a knowledge base for self-service, and multi-channel support — all without a hefty monthly bill.

Relentify Chat offers all of this for free. Here is how it compares to Olark.

## The simplicity question

Olark's selling point is simplicity. It does one thing — live chat — and does it without bloat. For businesses that genuinely only need a chat box on their website with human agents responding, Olark delivers a clean, focused experience.

The question is whether that simplicity justifies $29/seat/month when Relentify Chat offers the same chat experience plus additional features for free.

Relentify Chat is not complex. The core chat experience is just as straightforward — visitors click the widget, type a message, and either an AI or a human agent responds. The additional features (knowledge base, ticketing, multi-channel) are there when you need them but do not clutter the basic chat workflow.

## AI responses

This is the most significant difference. Olark is explicitly human-only — they do not offer chatbots or AI responses. Their philosophy is that human conversation is better than automated responses.

There is truth in that philosophy. Human agents provide empathy, nuance, and understanding that AI cannot fully replicate. But there are practical realities:

- Small businesses cannot staff live chat 24/7
- Common questions (pricing, opening hours, return policies) do not require human nuance
- Visitors who arrive outside business hours leave if they cannot get an immediate answer
- Agents spend significant time answering the same questions repeatedly

Relentify Chat's AI handles common queries automatically, freeing human agents for conversations that actually benefit from the human touch. When the AI cannot answer, it seamlessly hands off to a human agent. When no agents are available, the AI provides helpful responses rather than showing an offline form.

This hybrid approach — AI for routine queries, humans for complex ones — serves customers better than either approach alone.

Olark integrates with popular CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), helpdesks (Zendesk, Freshdesk), and e-commerce platforms (Shopify, BigCommerce). Since Olark does not include its own ticketing or CRM, these integrations are important for connecting chat to your broader support workflow.

## Accessibility

Olark has invested in chat accessibility, supporting screen readers and keyboard navigation. Their PowerUp for accessibility compliance is one of their differentiators, particularly for businesses that must meet WCAG requirements.

Relentify Chat follows accessibility best practices with keyboard navigation and screen reader support. For businesses with strict WCAG compliance requirements, Olark's dedicated accessibility focus may be relevant.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Chat if:**
- You want live chat features for free rather than $29/seat/month
