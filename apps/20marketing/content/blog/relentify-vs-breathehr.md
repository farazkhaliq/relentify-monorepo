BreatheHR (now simply Breathe) is one of the UK's most established HR software platforms for small and medium businesses. It focuses on making people management simpler — with modules covering holiday management, sickness tracking, document storage, performance reviews, and employee onboarding.

Breathe has built a strong reputation in the UK SME market, and it is often the first HR platform that growing businesses consider. But its pricing structure — based on employee count tiers rather than per-employee pricing — and some feature limitations may mean you pay more than necessary or find gaps in what is included.

Relentify HR offers a competitive alternative with per-employee pricing, a broader feature set, and native integration with timesheet and accounting tools.

## Pricing

Breathe uses tiered pricing based on employee count ranges, while Relentify uses straightforward per-employee pricing.

| Employees | Relentify HR | Breathe |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| 1-10 | From £3/employee/month (£30 max) | From £13/month (flat) |
| 11-25 | From £3/employee/month (£75 max) | From £18/month (flat) |
| 26-50 | From £3/employee/month (£150 max) | From £72/month (flat) |
| 51-100 | From £3/employee/month (£300 max) | From £144/month (flat) |
| 100+ | From £3/employee/month | Custom |

At very small team sizes (under 10), Breathe's flat tier pricing can be competitive. But as you grow into the mid-range (25-100 employees), the comparison shifts. And importantly, Breathe's pricing above covers only the core module — add-on modules cost extra.

Relentify HR includes all features in the per-employee price. No modules to add, no features to unlock.
