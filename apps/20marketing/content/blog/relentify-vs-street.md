Street.co.uk positioned itself as a disruptor in the estate agent CRM market, offering a modern interface and transparent pricing at a time when most competitors hid their costs behind sales calls. It has gained traction with independent agents who appreciate the straightforward approach.

Relentify CRM takes a similar philosophy — transparent pricing, modern design, no hidden fees — while offering a broader feature set and a lower price point.

## Pricing comparison

Street.co.uk charges per branch with different tiers for sales, lettings, or combined. Pricing starts from around £200 per branch per month for the standard package.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Street.co.uk |
|---|---|---|
| Pricing model | Flat monthly rate | Per-branch |
| Starting price | £49/month | ~£200/branch/month |
| Contract | Monthly rolling | Monthly rolling |
| User seats | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Setup fee | None | None |
| Portal integration | Included | Included |
| Sales tools | Yes | Yes |
| Lettings tools | Yes | Yes |

Both platforms offer monthly rolling contracts, which is a shared advantage over competitors that lock agents into annual commitments. The price difference is significant — Relentify CRM costs roughly a quarter of Street.co.uk for comparable functionality.

## Sales features

Street.co.uk was built with a strong focus on the sales side of estate agency. It offers market appraisal tools, pipeline management, vendor reporting, and chain tracking.

Relentify CRM provides sales pipeline management, property listings, viewing management, and offer tracking. The sales toolset covers what independent agents need, though agencies with high-volume sales operations may find Street.co.uk's sales-specific features more refined.

## Lettings features

Both platforms cover the lettings lifecycle, but the depth differs.

| Feature | Relentify CRM | Street.co.uk |
|---|---|---|
| Tenancy management | Full lifecycle | Full lifecycle |
| Rent collection tracking | Automated | Automated |
| Arrears management | Built-in alerts | Built-in |
| Compliance tracking | Automated reminders | Automated |
| Maintenance requests | Built-in workflow | Built-in |
| Renewal management | Automated | Automated |
| Void period analysis | Yes | Limited |
| Landlord portfolio reports | Yes | Basic |

For lettings-focused agencies, Relentify CRM offers more analytical depth: void period tracking, landlord revenue analysis, and portfolio performance reporting.

## Contact management

Street.co.uk provides contact management with a modern interface, communication history, and the ability to track relationships across transactions.

Relentify CRM offers comparable contact management with custom fields, segmentation, automated follow-ups, and a unified communication log. The contact interfaces are similarly modern, reflecting both platforms' focus on usability.

## Reporting and dashboards

Street.co.uk offers real-time dashboards and reporting that cover pipeline status, team performance, and revenue metrics. The interface is clean and the data is presented clearly.

Relentify CRM provides similar dashboard functionality with the addition of deeper analytics: conversion funnel analysis, void period tracking, revenue by property type or landlord, and custom report creation.

## Portal integration

Both platforms integrate with major UK property portals. The portal feed management is comparable, with straightforward listing and updating.

## Mobile experience

Street.co.uk offers a mobile-friendly interface that works well on phones and tablets. It was designed as a cloud-first platform, and the mobile experience reflects that.

Relentify CRM's responsive design provides a similar mobile experience. Both platforms recognise that agents spend significant time away from their desks and need full functionality on mobile.

## Integrations

Street.co.uk integrates with a range of property industry tools: referencing services, compliance platforms, and accounting software.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify's accounting, inventory, and communication platforms. For agencies that want everything on one platform, this native integration is a significant advantage over connecting separate tools through integrations.

## Market appraisals

Street.co.uk offers market appraisal tools that help agents prepare professional presentations for potential vendors. This is a strong feature for sales-focused agencies competing for instructions.

Relentify CRM provides property valuation tracking and presentation tools, though the market appraisal feature is less developed than Street.co.uk's offering. For agencies where winning sales instructions is the primary goal, this is worth considering.

## Automation

Both platforms offer workflow automation. Street.co.uk provides automated communications triggered by pipeline stages, and Relentify CRM offers similar automation for follow-ups, compliance reminders, and tenant communications.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Street.co.uk if** you are primarily a sales agency where winning vendor instructions is your main focus, you need polished market appraisal tools, and the per-branch pricing is within your budget.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you want similar core functionality at a significantly lower price, you are lettings-focused and need deeper portfolio analytics, you want native integration with accounting and inventory tools, or you manage multiple branches and the per-branch pricing of alternatives adds up quickly.

## The bottom line

Street.co.uk deserves credit for bringing modern design and transparent pricing to a market that traditionally hid behind sales calls and annual contracts. It is a good product, particularly for sales-focused agencies.

But at roughly four times the price of Relentify CRM, the value equation favours the challenger. Relentify CRM provides comparable core features, stronger lettings analytics, and native integration with a broader business platform — all at a fraction of the cost. For independent agents and lettings specialists, the choice is clear.
