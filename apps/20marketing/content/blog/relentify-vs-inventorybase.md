InventoryBase has established itself as the leading property inventory software in the UK, used by thousands of inventory clerks, letting agents, and landlords. It provides a solid platform for creating check-in reports, check-out reports, mid-tenancy inspections, and condition schedules. For many in the industry, InventoryBase is the default choice.

But default choices deserve scrutiny. Relentify Inventories offers a more comprehensive feature set at a lower price, with the added advantage of native integration with CRM and accounting tools that inventory professionals increasingly need.

## Pricing comparison

InventoryBase operates on a per-report pricing model, with monthly subscription tiers that include a set number of reports. Additional reports cost extra.

| Feature | Relentify Inventories | InventoryBase (Professional) |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly price | £29/month | £39/month |
| Reports included | Unlimited | 30/month |
| Additional report cost | None | £1.50+ per report |
| User seats | Unlimited | 1 (additional seats extra) |
| Cloud storage | Unlimited | Limited by plan |
| Photo storage | Unlimited | Included |
| Branded reports | Included | Professional plan |
| Digital signatures | Via Relentify eSign | Add-on |

The pricing difference is significant for busy inventory clerks. On InventoryBase's Professional plan at £39 per month, you get 30 reports. If you complete 50 reports in a month, you pay extra for the additional 20. On Relentify Inventories, all reports are unlimited for £29 per month.

For a clerk completing 80 reports per month, the difference in annual cost is substantial — potentially hundreds of pounds saved with Relentify Inventories.

## Photo management

Photography is central to property inventory work, and both platforms handle it well.

Relentify Inventories provides the same photo capabilities: capture, annotation, timestamping, and geotagging. Additionally, Relentify Inventories supports barcode and QR code scanning for item identification and offers unlimited photo storage on every plan.

## Condition ratings

Both platforms use standardised condition rating scales for documenting the state of properties, rooms, and items. This standardisation is important for deposit dispute resolution, where clear condition documentation can determine outcomes.

Relentify Inventories uses a customisable rating scale that can be adapted to match the conventions used by different inventory companies and dispute resolution services.

## Report generation and delivery

InventoryBase generates PDF reports that can be branded with your company logo and details. Reports can be shared with landlords, agents, and tenants via email or through the InventoryBase portal.

Relentify Inventories generates branded PDF reports with the same sharing options, plus the ability to integrate with Relentify CRM for automatic report delivery to linked landlords and agents. This integration means reports flow directly into the property management workflow rather than being sent as standalone documents.

## Booking and scheduling

InventoryBase includes booking management features that help inventory clerks manage their appointment schedule. This is useful for independent clerks and small inventory companies.

Relentify Inventories includes appointment scheduling with calendar integration, automated reminders for upcoming appointments, and the ability to manage multiple clerks' schedules from a single dashboard. For inventory companies with multiple field workers, this scheduling depth makes a difference.

## Offline capability

Both platforms support offline work, which is essential for inventory clerks who frequently work in properties with poor mobile signal.

InventoryBase's mobile app allows offline report creation with synchronisation when connectivity returns. Relentify Inventories provides the same offline capability, with reports, photos, and notes all captured locally and synced automatically.

## Templates and customisation

InventoryBase offers report templates that can be customised with your branding and preferred report structure. Room templates and item lists help speed up the report creation process.

Relentify Inventories provides customisable templates with the addition of reusable room and item libraries that build up over time, reducing data entry for repeat properties. Custom sections, fields, and rating scales can be configured to match your specific workflow.

## Digital signatures

InventoryBase offers digital signature capture as part of the report workflow, though some features require specific plan tiers.

Relentify Inventories integrates with Relentify eSign for digital signature capture, providing legally binding electronic signatures on check-in and check-out reports. This is included in the plan price rather than requiring an additional subscription.

## Integration with the broader business

This is Relentify Inventories' most significant differentiator. InventoryBase is a standalone inventory tool — it creates reports but does not connect to your CRM, your accounting software, or your communication tools.

Relentify Inventories integrates natively with:

**Relentify CRM:** Inventory reports are linked directly to properties and tenancies in the CRM. When a check-out report identifies damage, the relevant landlord and tenant records are immediately accessible for dispute management.

**Relentify Accounting:** Invoice generation for inventory services can be automated based on completed reports. Fee tracking per landlord or agent is built into the accounting integration.

**Relentify eSign:** Tenancy agreements, check-in signatures, and other documents are managed through a single signing platform.

For inventory clerks who also manage their own billing, or letting agents who conduct inventories in-house, this integration eliminates the need for separate tools for reporting, invoicing, and property management.

## Mobile experience

Both platforms offer mobile apps designed for field use. InventoryBase's app is well-established and widely used, with a workflow optimised for the inventory creation process.

Relentify Inventories' mobile experience is designed with the same field-first approach: quick room creation, photo capture, condition rating, and item documentation. The interface is optimised for speed, which matters when you are documenting dozens of rooms and hundreds of items in a single visit.

## Comparative reports

Both platforms support comparative reports that overlay check-in and check-out data side by side. These reports are essential for deposit dispute resolution, showing the exact condition changes between the start and end of a tenancy.

Relentify Inventories' comparative reports include visual diff highlighting that makes it immediately clear which items or conditions have changed, reducing the time needed to prepare for dispute resolution.

## Who should choose which

**Choose InventoryBase if** you are an established inventory clerk with workflows built around InventoryBase, you do fewer than 30 reports per month (making the per-report model affordable), or you do not need CRM or accounting integration.

**Choose Relentify Inventories if** you do more than 30 reports per month and want unlimited reports at a lower price, you need your inventory reports to integrate with CRM and accounting, you want unlimited users without per-seat charges, or you are a letting agent who conducts inventories in-house and wants everything on one platform.

## The bottom line

InventoryBase is a good product that has served the UK inventory market well. Its mobile app is established, its report quality is professional, and its market position is strong.

But its per-report pricing model penalises busy clerks, its lack of business integration means inventory data lives in isolation, and its feature set has not evolved significantly in recent years.

Relentify Inventories offers more reports, more features, and deeper business integration for less money. For inventory professionals looking to reduce costs and streamline their workflow, it is the natural upgrade.
