Salesforce is the undisputed giant of the CRM world. With over 150,000 customers globally and an ecosystem of apps, consultants, and certifications, it is the platform that enterprises default to. Its capabilities are vast — there is almost nothing Salesforce cannot do if you have the budget and technical resources to make it happen.

But for estate and letting agents — businesses with specific, well-defined workflows and limited IT budgets — Salesforce is a sledgehammer for a nail. Relentify CRM provides the property-specific tools agents need without the complexity, cost, and consultant dependency that Salesforce demands.

## The cost reality

Salesforce's published pricing starts at £20 per user per month for its Starter edition, which sounds reasonable. But the Starter edition lacks the features most businesses need: workflow automation, custom objects, and advanced reporting require the Professional (£80/user/month) or Enterprise (£165/user/month) edition.

Then add implementation costs. Salesforce implementation for a small business typically runs from £5,000 to £50,000, depending on customisation requirements. Ongoing administration often requires a dedicated Salesforce administrator or consulting retainer.

For a ten-person letting agency, Salesforce Enterprise would cost approximately £19,800 per year in licenses alone — before implementation, customisation, and administration. Relentify CRM costs £588 per year for the same team with all features included.

## The customisation trap

Salesforce can be customised to do almost anything. This is both its greatest strength and its biggest risk for property businesses.

To make Salesforce work for estate agency, you need:

1. **Custom objects** for properties, tenancies, viewings, and maintenance requests
2. **Custom fields** on every object for property-specific data (EPC rating, gas safety expiry, deposit scheme, etc.)
3. **Custom workflows** for the lettings pipeline, compliance tracking, and tenant communications
4. **Custom dashboards** for property-specific reporting
5. **Third-party integrations** for portal feeds (if they even exist)
6. **Ongoing maintenance** as Salesforce's regular updates can break custom configurations

This customisation project requires either a Salesforce consultant (expensive) or an in-house Salesforce administrator (also expensive). And the result is a bespoke system that only your team understands, making future changes costly and risky.

Relentify CRM provides all of this natively. No customisation required, no consultants needed, no ongoing maintenance burden.

## Where Salesforce genuinely excels

Salesforce's strengths are real and significant for the businesses it targets:

- **Enterprise-scale operations** with thousands of users across multiple countries
- **Complex sales processes** with long cycles and multiple stakeholders
- **Deep analytics** with Einstein AI for predictive insights
- **Massive integration ecosystem** with thousands of AppExchange apps
- **Platform extensibility** for businesses that need truly custom applications

For a multinational property company with hundreds of employees, complex reporting requirements, and dedicated IT staff, Salesforce might be appropriate. For the vast majority of UK estate and letting agents, it is overkill.

## Implementation timeline

Salesforce implementations for small businesses typically take four to twelve weeks, involving requirements gathering, customisation, data migration, testing, and training.

Relentify CRM can be set up in a day. Import your data, configure your preferences, and start working. The interface is intuitive enough that formal training is rarely needed.

## Ongoing administration

Salesforce requires ongoing administration: managing user permissions, updating custom objects as needs change, maintaining integrations, and handling the three annual platform updates that can break customisations.

Relentify CRM requires no ongoing administration. Updates are automatic and do not break existing configurations. User management is straightforward, and the platform evolves based on property industry needs rather than generic enterprise requirements.

## Mobile experience

Salesforce's mobile app is comprehensive and well-designed, providing access to the full CRM on mobile devices.

Relentify CRM's responsive design provides full functionality on mobile. Both platforms deliver a good mobile experience, though Relentify's is designed specifically for the tasks agents perform on the move: property lookups, viewing confirmations, and tenant communications.

## Integration

Salesforce's AppExchange has thousands of integrations. For generic business tools (email, calendars, marketing platforms), the options are extensive.

Relentify CRM integrates natively with Relentify Accounting, Relentify Inventories, and Relentify's communication tools. For property businesses, this purpose-built integration is more valuable than access to thousands of generic apps.

## Who should choose which

**Choose Salesforce if** you are a large property company with more than 100 employees, you have dedicated IT staff or a Salesforce administrator, you need enterprise-grade customisation for complex multi-country operations, or you are already embedded in the Salesforce ecosystem.

**Choose Relentify CRM if** you are an independent agent or small chain (which describes the vast majority of UK agents), you do not have the budget for enterprise software and consultants, you need property-specific features that work from day one, or you want a platform you can set up in a day rather than a quarter.

## The bottom line

Salesforce is the most powerful CRM in the world. For enterprises with the budget and resources to unlock that power, it delivers extraordinary capability.

But power without purpose is just complexity. For estate and letting agents, Relentify CRM delivers the specific capabilities the industry needs — property management, tenancy tracking, compliance monitoring, portal integration — at a price that makes sense for small businesses.

The question is not whether Salesforce is a better CRM. It is whether a better CRM is what you actually need. For property professionals, the answer is a purpose-built platform that understands the industry. That is Relentify CRM.
