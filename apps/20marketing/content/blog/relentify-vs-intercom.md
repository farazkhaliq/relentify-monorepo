Intercom changed how businesses think about customer communication. It pioneered the conversational support model — replacing traditional ticketing with a messenger-first approach that feels more like chatting with a friend than submitting a support ticket.

But Intercom's pricing has become a real problem for growing businesses. What starts as a reasonable monthly cost can escalate dramatically as you add features, agents, and conversation volume. Many teams find themselves paying hundreds or thousands per month for capabilities that should be standard.

Relentify Connect offers the same conversational, multi-channel support experience — with transparent pricing, more features included in the base plan, and no surprise bills as your business grows.

## Pricing reality check

Intercom's pricing is famously complex. The headline per-seat price does not tell the full story.

| Cost factor | Relentify Connect | Intercom |
|-------------|------------------|----------|
| Starter plan | £12/agent/month | $39/seat/month (~£31) |
| Professional plan | £25/agent/month | $99/seat/month (~£78) |
| Enterprise plan | £45/agent/month | Custom pricing |
| AI/bot conversations | Included | $0.99 per resolution |
| Proactive messages | Included | Per-message charges on some plans |
| WhatsApp | Included | Add-on |
| Product tours | N/A | $199/month add-on |
| Hidden costs | None | Resolution fees, add-ons, overages |

The per-resolution charge for Intercom's AI bot (Fin) is the most discussed hidden cost. If Fin resolves 500 conversations per month, that is an additional $495 on top of your seat costs. For businesses with high support volume, this quickly becomes the largest line item on the bill.

Relentify Connect includes AI-powered responses in the plan price. There are no per-resolution fees, no per-message charges, and no surprises.

## Conversational support vs traditional ticketing

Intercom popularised the idea that support should feel like a conversation, not a ticket queue. Their messenger interface is clean, fast, and familiar to users.

Relentify Connect takes the same approach. Conversations flow naturally, with context preserved across channels. If a customer starts on live chat and follows up by email, the entire history is in one thread. Agents see the full picture without switching between systems.

The difference is that Relentify also supports traditional ticketing for teams that need it. Not every support interaction is a quick chat — some require investigation, escalation, and structured workflows. Relentify handles both conversational and ticket-based support in a single platform.

## AI and bots

Both platforms invest heavily in AI, but the business models could not be more different.

**Intercom's Fin** is a powerful AI agent that can resolve customer queries by searching your knowledge base and generating responses. It works well. The problem is the pricing: $0.99 per resolution adds up quickly and unpredictably. A spike in support volume — say, after a product launch or during an outage — can result in a surprise bill.

**Relentify Connect's AI** offers similar capabilities — knowledge base-powered responses, suggested replies for agents, automatic categorisation, and conversation summaries — all included in the plan price. Your cost does not change based on how many conversations the AI handles.

For businesses that want to leverage AI to reduce support workload without worrying about variable costs, this is a significant advantage.

**Intercom** offers Series (visual workflow builder) and custom bots for automating customer interactions. These are powerful tools, but the full workflow builder is available only on higher-tier plans.

**Relentify Connect** provides a workflow automation engine on all plans. You can create rules that trigger on conversation events, route conversations based on criteria, send automatic responses, and escalate based on SLA timers — without paying for a premium tier.

Intercom's reporting is comprehensive on higher tiers. The Starter plan offers basic metrics, while detailed analytics — including custom reports, team performance comparisons, and trend analysis — require the Professional or higher plan.

Relentify Connect includes full reporting from the Starter plan:

Switching platforms can feel daunting, especially if you have years of conversation history. Relentify Connect supports importing conversation history, contact data, and knowledge base articles from Intercom, so you do not lose context.

The migration process preserves conversation threads and customer records, allowing your team to pick up where they left off.

**Choose Relentify Connect if:**
- Predictable pricing matters more than variable AI costs
