Help Scout has built a devoted following by doing one thing well: making helpdesk software feel simple. Its shared inbox approach strips away the complexity of traditional ticketing systems, making it approachable for small teams that do not want to spend weeks learning a new tool.

That simplicity is genuinely appealing. But as your team grows, you may find that Help Scout's simplicity comes at the expense of features you actually need — and that those features are only available at a significant price premium.

Relentify Connect aims to deliver the same clean, intuitive experience while including more capabilities at every price point.

## The shared inbox model

Relentify Connect uses the same shared inbox model. Conversations from all channels — email, chat, social media, messaging apps — appear in a unified inbox. Each conversation is a thread with full history, internal notes, and assignment tracking.

The experience is comparable. Both platforms make it easy for a new agent to start responding to customers within minutes of logging in.

Relentify Connect provides a more powerful automation engine:

## Beacon vs Relentify Chat widget

Help Scout's Beacon is a versatile widget that combines live chat, knowledge base search, and contact forms. It is well-designed and integrates seamlessly with Help Scout's inbox.

Relentify's chat widget offers similar functionality — live chat, knowledge base integration, and pre-chat forms — plus AI-powered responses that can resolve common queries automatically before they reach an agent.

Both widgets are customisable in terms of appearance and positioning. The key difference is that Relentify's widget includes AI capabilities without an additional charge.

## Customer profiles

Both platforms maintain customer profiles with conversation history, making it easy for agents to understand context.

Help Scout's customer sidebar shows contact information, previous conversations, and data from integrations. Relentify Connect provides similar customer profiles, with the addition of activity timelines that show interactions across all channels.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Connect if:**
- You want the simplicity of a shared inbox with more features included
- Multi-channel support (social, messaging) matters
- SLA management and CSAT at a lower price point is important
- You want AI features without upgrading to a premium plan

- Simplicity is your top priority and you do not need social or messaging channels
