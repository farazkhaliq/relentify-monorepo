Tidio has become one of the most popular live chat and chatbot platforms for small businesses and e-commerce sites. Its free tier attracts users, and its chatbot builder gives businesses a way to automate conversations without coding. It is a well-designed product that has found a strong market fit.

But Tidio's free tier has meaningful limitations — and the paid plans can be expensive when you add the features you actually need. Relentify Chat offers a more generous free tier with AI-powered responses, a knowledge base, and no conversation limits.

Here is how the two compare.

## Pricing

| Plan | Relentify Chat | Tidio |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Free tier | Yes (unlimited agents) | Yes (50 conversations/month) |
| Starter | Free | $29/month |
| Chatbot/AI | Included free | From $29/month (Chatbots) or $39/month (Tidio+) |
| Premium | Free | $749/month (Tidio+ Premium) |

Tidio's free tier is capped at 50 live conversations per month. For a website with even moderate traffic, this limit is reached within the first week or two. Once you hit it, visitors see a contact form instead of live chat — which defeats the purpose.

Relentify Chat has no conversation limits on any tier, including the free one. You could handle 50 or 5,000 conversations per month at no cost.

Tidio's free tier is designed as a funnel to paid plans — the 50-conversation limit ensures that any business with real traffic needs to upgrade. Relentify Chat's free tier is designed to be genuinely usable long-term.

## AI and chatbots

This is where the two platforms diverge most significantly.

**Tidio** offers chatbot templates and a visual flow builder. You create conversation paths with triggers, conditions, and responses. The chatbot builder is well-designed and intuitive — one of Tidio's best features. However, the Chatbots plan starts at $29/month, and the more advanced Tidio+ AI starts at $39/month.

**Relentify Chat** takes a different approach. Instead of a flow-based chatbot, it uses AI powered by your knowledge base. You write help articles, the AI reads them, and it answers customer questions in natural language. There is no flow to build, no decision trees to maintain — the AI understands context and generates appropriate responses.

| AI/Bot feature | Relentify Chat | Tidio |
|----------------|---------------|-------|
| Approach | AI knowledge base | Visual chatbot builder |
| Setup time | Minutes (write articles) | Hours (build flows) |
| Maintenance | Update articles | Update flows |
| Natural language | Yes | Limited |
| 24/7 responses | Yes (free) | $29+/month |
| Complex queries | Handles context | Follows predefined paths |
| Cost | Free | From $29/month |

Both approaches have merit. Tidio's flow builder gives you precise control over conversation paths, which is valuable for structured processes like lead qualification or order tracking. Relentify's AI approach is simpler to set up and maintains itself as you update your knowledge base.

For most small businesses that want automated answers to common questions, the AI approach is easier and more effective.

## E-commerce features

Tidio has invested heavily in e-commerce integrations, particularly with Shopify. Features include:
- Cart abandonment recovery bots
- Product recommendation bots
- Order status tracking
- Shopify data in agent dashboard

Relentify Chat does not focus as heavily on e-commerce. For online retailers using Shopify, Tidio's e-commerce-specific features provide genuine value that Relentify does not match.

For non-e-commerce businesses — service companies, SaaS, agencies, property businesses — these features are irrelevant, and Relentify's broader feature set at no cost is the better choice.

## Knowledge base

Relentify Chat includes a knowledge base that serves dual purpose: self-service for customers and a data source for AI responses. Tidio does not include a knowledge base — customers either chat with a human, interact with a chatbot flow, or nothing.

For businesses that want to reduce support volume by enabling self-service, the knowledge base is a valuable inclusion that Tidio lacks.

## Who should choose which?

**Choose Relentify Chat if:**
- You want unlimited conversations and agents for free
- AI-powered responses without building chatbot flows appeal to you
- A knowledge base that feeds AI answers is valuable
- You need WhatsApp support
- You are not primarily an e-commerce business

**Choose Tidio if:**
- You run a Shopify or e-commerce store and want deep integration
- You prefer visual chatbot builders with precise flow control
- Cart abandonment and product recommendation bots matter
- You specifically need Tidio's e-commerce automations

## The bottom line

Tidio is excellent for e-commerce businesses that want chatbot automation with deep Shopify integration. If that describes your business, Tidio's paid plans deliver real value.

For everyone else — service businesses, SaaS, agencies, property companies, and any business that wants professional live chat with AI responses — Relentify Chat offers more features for free than Tidio's paid plans provide.

Unlimited conversations, unlimited agents, AI responses, knowledge base, full reporting, and no branding watermark. All free.
