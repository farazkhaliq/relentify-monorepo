## What this does

Lets you update the core details about your company that appear on invoices, reports, and HMRC submissions. This includes your registered name, address, VAT registration number, financial year end date, and bank details for remittance advice.

## When to use it

Update these settings when you first set up your account, when your company details change, or when you register for VAT.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Settings** and select **Company Settings**
2. Update your **Company Name** if it has changed
3. Enter or edit your **Registered Address**
4. Add or update your **VAT Number**
5. Set your **Financial Year End** date (e.g. 31 March or 31 December)
6. Enter your **Bank Details** (sort code and account number) for remittance advice on invoices
7. Click **Save Changes**
8. The updated details will appear on all future invoices and reports
