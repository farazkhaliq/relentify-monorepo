## What this does

Shows a chronological log of every significant action taken in your account, including who made the change, what was changed, and the exact date and time. This is essential for compliance and for tracking down unexpected changes.

## When to use it

Review the audit log when you need to investigate a discrepancy, prepare for an audit, or check what a team member has done. Available on the corporate tier.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Audit Log** from the main navigation
2. The log displays the most recent actions at the top
3. Use the **Date Range** filter to narrow down the time period
4. Use the **User** filter to see actions by a specific team member
5. Use the **Action Type** filter to show only specific events (e.g. created, updated, deleted)
6. Click any log entry to see the full details of the change
7. Use **Export** to download the log for your records or your auditor
