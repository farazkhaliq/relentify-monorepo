## What this does

Displays a cash flow statement broken into three sections: operating activities (day-to-day trade), investing activities (assets bought or sold), and financing activities (loans, equity). It shows your net cash movement for the period.

## When to use it

Run this report to understand whether your business is generating or consuming cash, even if the profit and loss report shows a profit.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Reports** and select **Cash Flow**
2. Set the **Period** using the date picker
3. Click **Run Report**
4. Review the **Operating Activities** section for cash from trading
5. Review the **Investing Activities** section for asset purchases or sales
6. Review the **Financing Activities** section for loans and repayments
7. Check the **Net Cash Movement** at the bottom
8. Click any line item to drill down into the underlying transactions
