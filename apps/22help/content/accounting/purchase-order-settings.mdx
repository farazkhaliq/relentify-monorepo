## What this does

Purchase order settings let you turn the PO approval workflow on or off and control who approves orders for each staff member. When enabled, purchase orders must be submitted and approved before they can be sent to suppliers.

## When to use it

Set this up when you first start using purchase orders, or whenever you need to change who is responsible for approving orders.

## Step by step

1. Go to **Settings** from the left-hand menu.
2. Find the **Purchase Orders** section.
3. Toggle **Require approval for purchase orders** on to enable the approval workflow.
4. Once enabled, you can set **approver mappings** for individual staff members.
5. To add a mapping, select a **staff member** and assign their **approver** from the dropdown.
6. If a staff member does not have a specific approver assigned, the entity default approver will be used.
7. Click **Save** to apply your changes.
8. To disable the approval workflow later, toggle the setting off. New POs will skip the approval step entirely.
