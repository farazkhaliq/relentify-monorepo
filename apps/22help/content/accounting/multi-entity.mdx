## What this does

Allows you to run more than one business from a single login. Each organisation (entity) has its own chart of accounts, transactions, invoices, and reports, completely separate from the others.

## When to use it

Use multi-entity if you operate more than one limited company, trade under different names, or need to keep a property business separate from a trading business.

## Step by step

1. Click your **organisation name** in the top bar to open the entity switcher
2. Click **Create New Organisation**
3. Enter the **Company Name** and **Registered Address**
4. Set the **Financial Year End** and **VAT Number** if applicable
5. Click **Create**
6. The new organisation opens with a default chart of accounts ready to use
7. To switch back, click the organisation name in the top bar and select the one you need
8. Each organisation's data is completely separate and will not mix with the others
